Kadupul Flower (Epiphyllum oxypetalum):

The "Queen of the Night," the kadupul flower is native to Sri Lanka and blooms only at night. What makes it particularly rare is its incredibly short lifespan – the flower wilts before dawn, making it nearly impossible to witness in full bloom. By Cristina K.C on April 1, 2024

Middlemist's Red (Middlemist camellia)

With only two known specimens in existence – one in New Zealand and one in the United Kingdom – Middlemist's Red is considered one of the rarest flowers in the world. It boasts stunning, deep red blooms and is highly prized by collectors. By Cristina K.C Fox on April 1, 2024

Youtan Poluo

This tiny, translucent flower measures less than 1 millimeter in diameter and was thought to be extinct until its rediscovery in 2010. Found primarily in China, Youtan Poluo holds great cultural significance in Buddhist teachings, symbolizing purity and enlightenment. By Cristina K.C on April 1, 2024
